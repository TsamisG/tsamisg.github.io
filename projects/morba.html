Multi-Objective Reward-based Algorithms for the Complete Coverage Path Planning Problem on Arbitrary Grids

This project was conducted as my Thesis under the supervision of Professor Georgios Chamilothoris, in the MSc program "Advanced Product Design, Engineering & Manufacturing" offered by Kingston University and University of West Attica. In the PDF file below, a synopsis of the Thesis in the form of a two-column paper can be found.
A part of this project was presented in the 1st International Conference on Frontiers of Artificial Intelligence, Ethics, and Multidisciplinary Applications, which took place on the 25th and the 26th of September 2023 in Athens, Greece (FAIEMA 2023), following peer review. The conference proceedings will be published as an edited book volume with the Springer book series: Frontiers of Artificial Intelligence, Ethics and Multidisciplinary Applications (Electronic ISSN 2731-8133, Print ISSN 2731-8125).
